Built for real progress

Three core systems, all wired into one cute, evolving mascot.

📸

AI Posture Scan

Snap a front and side photo. Get a Shell Score (0–100), pinpointed problem zones, and a personalized drill plan. Your photos are analyzed and discarded — never stored.

🎧

AirPods Posture Sessions

Start a session and your AirPods track head orientation in real time. See a live posture score and a full session summary when you're done. Works on AirPods Pro, Max, and 3rd gen.

⚡

XP & Evolution

Every check-in, scan, drill, and good-posture hour earns XP. Hit thresholds and evolve your shrimp through five stages, from curled rookie to crowned Stage 5.

🔥

Streaks & Daily Drills

Daily check-ins, guided training drills, and a streak system that actually feels good to keep alive. Gentle reminders, no nagging.

How it works

Three steps. No willpower required.

1

Sign in & hatch

Sign in with Apple or Google, set up your profile, choose a shell color, and meet the curled-up little guy who needs your help.

2

Scan, monitor, drill

Run a quick AI posture scan, start AirPods sessions when you're at your desk, and finish daily drills. Everything earns XP.

3

Evolve & stand tall

Build streaks, hit XP thresholds, and watch your shrimp evolve through five stages — from curled to crowned.
