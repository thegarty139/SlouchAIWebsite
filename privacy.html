Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us

Your posture data stays on your device. We believe in privacy-first design.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera and Posture Data

Slouch AI uses your device's camera to monitor posture in real-time. This data is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers without your explicit consent
  • Used solely for posture detection and improvement recommendations

1.2 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Authentication tokens from Apple/Google Sign-In

1.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymous analytics to improve our service:

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash reports and performance metrics

1.4 Health and Fitness Data

With your permission, we may access:

  • Posture tracking history
  • Exercise completion data
  • Progress metrics and streaks

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide real-time posture monitoring and alerts
  • Generate personalized exercise recommendations
  • Track your progress and maintain streaks
  • Sync data across your devices (when logged in)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important service updates

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Core Data for structured information
  • Secure keychain for sensitive data
  • Device-level encryption

3.2 Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using TLS
  • Stored securely in Firebase Firestore
  • Accessible only with your authentication
  • Can be deleted at any time from settings

3.3 AI Processing

When using AI features:

  • Posture images are processed locally when possible
  • AI coaching requests are anonymized
  • No personal health data is retained by AI providers

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Analytics

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking
  • Crashlytics: Crash reporting and diagnostics

4.2 Authentication

  • Firebase Auth: Secure user authentication
  • Apple Sign-In: Privacy-focused authentication
  • Google Sign-In: Cross-platform authentication

4.3 AI Services

  • OpenAI: Coaching recommendations (anonymized)
  • Anthropic Claude: Exercise suggestions (anonymized)

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

5.1 Access and Portability

  • Export all your data from the app settings
  • Request a copy of your cloud-stored data

5.2 Modification and Deletion

  • Edit or delete any personal information
  • Clear local posture history
  • Delete your account and all associated data

5.3 Permission Management

  • Revoke camera access at any time
  • Disable cloud sync
  • Opt out of analytics collection

6. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until app deletion or manual clearing
  • Cloud data: Retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Analytics: Anonymized and aggregated after 60 days

7. Children's Privacy

Slouch AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

If you use Slouch AI outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices:

  • Email: privacy@slouchai.app
  • In-app: Settings ’ Privacy ’ Contact Support
